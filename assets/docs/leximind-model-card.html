Model Card: LexiMind Predictive Diagnosis

By Leslie Amadi

Version: 1.0

Date: 9 November 2025

Overview

LexiMind predicts the likelihood of chronic illnesses based on patient data, supporting doctors in early diagnosis and treatment planning.

How it Works

  • Model Types: Logistic Regression, Random Forest, Neural Network
  • Input Data: Patient demographics, lab results, lifestyle data
  • Output: Risk probabilities for diabetes, heart disease, kidney disease

Performance

  • Accuracy: 91%
  • Reduced readmissions: 18%
  • Average time saved per doctor: 3 hours per 50 patients

Intended Use

  • Hospitals and clinics for early screening
  • Healthcare apps for patient monitoring

Limitations

  • Requires accurate and anonymized input data
  • Human review necessary for final diagnosis
  • May not generalize outside the trained population

Responsible AI

  • Data anonymization ensures patient privacy
  • Explainable AI for transparency
  • Model continuously retrained with new data
