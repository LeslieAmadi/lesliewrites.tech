# Understanding Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

## Introduction
Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) adds an extra layer of security to your online accounts.

## Why 2FA Matters
- Protects against stolen passwords  
- Reduces risk of account takeover  
- Adds peace of mind

## How to Set Up 2FA
1. Enable 2FA in your account settings.  
2. Choose an authentication method: SMS, authenticator app, or email.  
3. Follow prompts to link your device.

## Tips for Users
- Always have backup codes  
- Do not share your authentication method  
- Use authenticator apps for stronger security
