UX Writing Sample — Empty State Experience

A gentle, guiding empty state experience for a financial dashboard. Clear tone, calm emotion, and helpful next steps.

Empty State — “No Transactions Yet”

When users first open a financial dashboard, there’s no data to show. Instead of showing a blank page, we use this moment to build trust and help them take the right action.

UX Goal: Reassure the user, reduce confusion, and offer a clear next step without pressure.

Final UX Copy

No activity yet

When you make your first payment or transfer, your activity will appear here.

Make a transfer

Design Principles

  • Human tone: Calm and friendly, not technical.
  • Reassurance: “Activity will appear here” prevents confusion.
  • Single CTA: Avoids overwhelming the user.
