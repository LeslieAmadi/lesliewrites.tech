Full UX Writing Pack – Gaming Product

A complete UX microcopy system designed for gaming environments, focused on clarity, accessibility, regulatory safety, and high-quality user experience.

Overview

This UX Writing Pack provides the complete microcopy framework for in-game messaging across gameplay, menus, onboarding flows, notifications, error handling, and accessibility support. Designed to be thematic (Asian Mythology: “Crimson Temple”) while maintaining global clarity and compliance.

Core UX Writing Principles

  • Clarity First: Players should instantly understand the action or outcome.
  • Tonal Harmony: All copy supports a ceremonial, mystical tone without confusing users.
  • Compliance Safe: No promises, no exaggerations, no implied guarantees.
  • Accessibility: Screen-reader friendly, concise, structured.
  • Confidence: Microcopy guides players calmly through errors or uncertainty.

Notifications

Short, direct messages that keep players informed without disrupting gameplay.

Positive Notifications

  • “A blessing has been added to your balance.”
  • “Your wager has been accepted.”
  • “Feature activated successfully.”
  • “Session reconnected.”

Neutral / System Notifications

  • “Your settings have been saved.”
  • “Loading game data…”
  • “Reconnecting to the temple…”
  • “Results retrieved.”

Warnings

  • “Your balance is low.”
  • “Auto-Play stopped due to limits reached.”
  • “Some features may be unavailable.”

Error States

Clear and recovery-oriented, free of thematic exaggeration and compliant in all regions.

  • Connection Lost: “Connection lost. Attempting to reconnect…”
  • Reconnect Failed: “Unable to reconnect. Returning to lobby.”
  • RNG Error: “A system error occurred. Please try again.”
  • Insufficient Funds: “Balance too low for this bet.”
  • Feature Recovery: “Your feature is being restored. Please wait…”
  • Session Expired: “Your session has ended. Please log in again.”
  • Unexpected Error: “Something went wrong. Try again shortly.”

Modal Dialogues

Confirmation Modals

  • “Are you sure you want to exit the game?”
  • “Start Auto-Play with these settings?”
  • “Apply new bet size?”

Feature Modals (Thematic)

  • “The temple gates open. Your Free Spins await.”
  • “You have summoned the Crimson Fortune Wheel.”
  • “A sacred prize has been unlocked.”

Responsible Gaming Modals

  • “You have reached your time limit. Please take a break.”
  • “Your loss limit has been reached. Auto-Play has been disabled.”
  • “Please confirm you want to continue playing.”

Empty States

No Wins

“The temple rests. Try again to awaken its spirit.”

No History

“Your journey begins here. Play a round to record your path.”

No Features Triggered

“No feature results available yet.”

No Connection

“Unable to load data. Check your connection.”

Tooltips

  • Bet Slider: “Adjust your bet per spin.”
  • Auto-Play: “Set automatic spins and limits.”
  • Paytable: “See symbol values and feature rules.”
  • Scatter Symbol: “3+ Scatters trigger the Bonus Wheel.”
  • Spirit Meter: “Fill the meter to guarantee a feature.”

Onboarding & Tutorials

Simple, high-impact onboarding text for new players.

Step 1 — Bet Introduction

“Choose your bet. You can adjust it anytime.”

Step 2 — Game Goal

“Match symbols across the reels to win.”

Step 3 — Bonus Mechanics

“Collect Scatters to unlock powerful features.”

Step 4 — Responsible Gaming

“Set limits and play responsibly.”

Accessibility Microcopy

  • Screen-reader labels
    • “Spin button”
    • “Increase bet”
    • “Decrease bet”
    • “Open menu”
  • Alt text examples
    • “Crimson temple backdrop with golden lanterns.”
    • “Dragon symbol glowing with fire.”
  • Reduced Motion Text “Animations disabled for accessibility.”
  • Colour Blind Mode Toggle “Enable enhanced contrast.”

Responsible Gaming Copy

  • “Remember to take regular breaks.”
  • “Set betting limits to stay in control.”
  • “If the fun stops, stop.”
  • “You are playing in a safe, regulated environment.”

System Messages

  • “Game saved successfully.”
  • “Audio settings updated.”
  • “Downloading updates…”
  • “Server maintenance in progress.”
