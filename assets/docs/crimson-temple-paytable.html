Detailed paytable, symbol hierarchy, feature payouts, and math-facing documentation for the Crimson Temple online slot (Asian mythology theme).

1. Overview

This document defines the full paytable and symbol design for the Crimson Temple video slot. It is intended for:

  • Game Designers – to align on symbol hierarchy and thematic intent.
  • Math / RNG Engineers – to implement symbol values and win logic.
  • Front-End Developers – to wire UI, paytable screen, and tooltips.
  • Compliance & Certification Teams – to validate payout structures per jurisdiction.

The paytable has been designed to support a medium–high volatility profile and an overall target RTP of 96.10% (base version), combining steady low-symbol wins with high-impact feature potential from the Free Spins and Crimson Fortune Wheel.

2. Base Game Structure

  • Grid: 5 reels × 3 rows
  • Paylines: 25 fixed paylines
  • Win Direction: Left to right only
  • Minimum Bet: 0.10 (currency-agnostic)
  • Maximum Bet: 50.00
  • Volatility: Medium–High

All line wins are multiplied by the line bet (total bet ÷ 25). Only the highest win per line is paid. Simultaneous wins on different lines are added.

3. Symbol Tiers & Thematic Roles

Symbols are grouped into four tiers: Premium Characters, High Symbols, Mid Symbols, and Low Symbols. In addition, there are Special Symbols that drive features.

5. Special Symbols & Feature Payouts

5.1 Wild Symbol – Crimson Sigil

  • Symbol Name: Crimson Sigil
  • Function: Substitutes for all paying symbols except Scatter and Bonus.
  • Appearance: Reels 2, 3, 4, and 5 only.
  • Pays: No standalone payout; only contributes to line wins.

5.2 Scatter Symbol – Temple Bell (Free Spins)

  • Symbol Name: Temple Bell
  • Trigger: 3 or more Scatters anywhere in view.
  • Base Payout: Scatter wins are multiplied by total bet.
Scatters Award
3 2× total bet + 10 Free Spins
4 5× total bet + 15 Free Spins
5 20× total bet + 20 Free Spins

5.3 Bonus Symbol – Crimson Coin (Bonus Wheel)

  • Symbol Name: Crimson Coin
  • Trigger: 3+ Bonus symbols on reels 1, 3, and 5
  • Award: Entry to the Crimson Fortune Wheel feature.

The Bonus Symbol does not have a direct pay value; it unlocks the Crimson Fortune Wheel, where the player can win:

  • Fixed cash prizes (e.g. 20×, 40×, 80× total bet)
  • Additional Free Spins bundles
  • Feature upgrade (e.g. “Super Spins” with more Wilds)

6. Example Win Scenarios

6.1 Line Win Example (Base Game)

Total Bet = 2.50 → Line Bet = 2.50 ÷ 25 = 0.10.

Player hits 5× Lantern of Embers on a single payline:

Payout = 10× line bet = 10 × 0.10 = 1.00.

6.2 Combined Line Wins

Same spin:

  • 5× Lantern of Embers on Line 3 → 1.00
  • 4× Lotus Relic on Line 7 → 1.8 × 0.10 = 0.18
  • 3× A on Line 10 → 0.3 × 0.10 = 0.03

Total win for spin = 1.21.

6.3 Scatter Win + Free Spins

Total bet = 2.50; player hits 4 Temple Bells (Scatters).

  • Scatter payout = 5× total bet = 5 × 2.50 = 12.50
  • Feature: 15 Free Spins awarded

6.4 Bonus Wheel Outcome

Player triggers the Crimson Fortune Wheel and lands on 40× total bet.

Total bet = 2.50 → Wheel prize = 40 × 2.50 = 100.00.

7. RTP & Volatility Breakdown

The paytable and feature structure contribute to an overall target RTP of 96.10%. This can be split conceptually into:

  • Base Game Wins: ~62.0%
  • Free Spins Feature: ~24.5%
  • Crimson Fortune Wheel: ~9.6%

These values are illustrative and depend on the final math model implementation, including hit frequency, reel strips, and feature trigger rates.

7.1 Volatility Profile

  • Base Hit Frequency: Approx. 28–32%
  • Free Spins Hit Frequency: Approx. 1 in 140–180 spins
  • Bonus Wheel Hit Frequency: Approx. 1 in 200–250 spins

The combination of frequent small wins and occasional high-impact features positions the game in the medium–high volatility range. This should be confirmed by simulation and certified as part of the math model approval process.

8. Jurisdiction & Configuration Notes

  • Alternative RTP configurations (e.g. 94.10%, 92.00%) can be derived by adjusting: feature frequency, wheel prize weighting, or high-tier symbol frequencies.
  • Some jurisdictions may require:
    • Removal or rewording of certain thematic phrases.
    • Specific limitations on maximum win or feature chaining.
  • Paytable values must be reflected identically in:
    • In-game paytable UI
    • Help screen / info pages
    • Math specification document
    • Certification submissions

9. UI / UX Implementation Notes

  • All paytable values should be displayed as multipliers (x bet) for localisation simplicity.
  • Hover / tap tooltips for each symbol:
    • Crimson Priestess: “Highest paying symbol.”
    • Temple Bell: “Scatter – triggers Free Spins.”
    • Crimson Coin: “Bonus – unlocks the Crimson Fortune Wheel.”
  • Accessibility:
    • Symbols should have alt text or readable names for screen readers.
    • Paytable screen must be navigable via keyboard/focus order.
