- "No transactions yet. Start by adding your first payment!"  
- "You haven’t added any cards. Tap 'Add Card' to continue."

- "We couldn’t process your payment. Check your details and try again."  
- "Oops! Something went wrong. Refresh and try again."

- "Swipe left to see your spending summary."  
- "Tap here to quickly add a new transaction."

## Success Messages
- "Payment successfully completed!"  
- "Your settings have been updated."
