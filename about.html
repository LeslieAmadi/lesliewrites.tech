About Me 🌸

I'm Leslie — a technical writer working in the IT industry. I enjoy documenting complex systems because it lets me understand products from both the engineering and user perspective. My focus is on turning complexity into calm, structured documentation that supports confident decisions and safe technology.

Download CV Get in touch →

I'm especially interested in responsible AI, full-stack systems, and clear system design. You can probably tell a lot about me from the way this site looks — I'm playful but professional, and I wanted the site to express that balance.

My journey has shaped how I work. Born in Nigeria, I moved to Hungary at 15, later lived in Ukraine, and relocated to Germany during the war. Today I'm building a stable life here — learning German, making new friends, and growing into the next chapter of my career.

Outside of work I enjoy ice skating, cooking seafood from different cultures, reading, writing, and listening to podcasts. On weekends I often spend time catching up with my siblings and my mum back home — staying grounded in where I come from while building where I'm going.
